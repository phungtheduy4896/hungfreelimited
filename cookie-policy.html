Cookie usage, analytics, and advertising technology information.

Cookie Policy for HUNG FREE LIMITED, including analytics and Google Ads related cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on a device when a user visits a website. Similar technologies can also be used for analytics, advertising measurement, security, and user experience functions.

Why We Use Them

HUNG FREE LIMITED may use cookies and similar technologies to support essential website features, understand traffic, improve content, detect misuse, and measure advertising performance.

Cookie Categories

  • Essential cookies: help core site functionality and security
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand visits, pages viewed, and general user behaviour
  • Advertising cookies: may support Google Ads conversion tracking, remarketing, and campaign measurement
  • Preference cookies: may remember basic user choices where implemented

Google Advertising Technologies

Where enabled, Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to measure ad performance, limit repeated ads, and show more relevant advertising based on prior visits.

Examples may include Google Ads conversion tracking and remarketing related identifiers.

Managing Cookies

Users can control cookies through browser settings and, where available, through a cookie consent banner. Blocking some cookies may affect website functionality or measurement accuracy.

Consent

For non-essential cookies, especially analytics and advertising cookies, the recommended approach is to request consent before activation for users in relevant jurisdictions.

If you continue to use the website after being presented with available choices, that may be interpreted in accordance with the consent mechanism implemented on the site.
